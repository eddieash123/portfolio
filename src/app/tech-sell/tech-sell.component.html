TechSell

Login Page


The login page shows the two emails for the different users set up in the database. Each user has their own cart information. You can also continue as a guest by clicking the link on the bottom.

Home Page


The home page shows the six different categories of products in the database. Clicking on any one of them will take you to the product page, and filter out for only those items.

Products Page


The products page shows all the products in the databse, based on what is clicked from teh homepage, or what is typed into the search bar.

You can also filter these items by color and price, and add specified quantiteis to the users cart.

Cart Page


The users cart page offers them the ability to checkout, or to remove items from the cart.
